The Source

Mineral Sites in Uganda

Eastern Uganda (Busia & Tororo)

Primary source for Gold (Busia) and Limestone/Phosphates (Tororo). Artisanal and small-scale mining is predominant here, supporting the local construction industry.

Karamoja Region (Northeast)

Rich in Gold and Marble. This region has seen increased exploration activities recently, unlocking massive potential for high-grade minerals.

Western Uganda (Kilembe & Albertine)

Historically significant for Copper and Cobalt (Kasese). The Albertine Graben is also drawing attention for broader resource potential alongside oil discoveries.
